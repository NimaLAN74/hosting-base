## FR-1 Data Inventory
Document Eurostat, ENTSO-E, OSM and NUTS datasets with coverage, structure and caveats.

Specify conceptual and logical schemas, including fact/dimension tables.

Describe at least one forecasting, clustering and geo-enriched dataset structure.

## FR-5 Architecture Blueprint
Describe the target end-to-end architecture at a conceptual level.
